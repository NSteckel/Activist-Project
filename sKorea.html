Check out this interactive, multi-media map designed to highlight and honor the important stories of Margo Okazawa-Rey's demilitarization work in South Korea.

Margo Okazawa-Rey is an educator, writer, and activist. Her demilitarization work in South Korea speaks volumes on such important and complex issues as women's rights, American imperialism, racism, and other hegemonic transnational systems. This interactive map highlights parts of this work that aren't easily elevated within academic structures. As our opening quote describes, these "invisible" details are actually some of the most important and fruitful parts of the story.
